Welcome to the PGC3!

The Portland Geek Council of Commerce and Culture strives to unite and cultivate the Portland geeky community; those people who are enthusiastic and, perhaps, a little obsessive in the pursuit of their passions. We will provide opportunities and resources to facilitate networking, community building, and targeted marketing, especially for our member organizations, but also for Portland's geek community at large.
